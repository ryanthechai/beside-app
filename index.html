Our Mission

Beside was created by parents who believe that the best gift we can give our children is our presence and attention. In a world of distractions, we wanted to create something that brings families back to what matters most: meaningful conversation and genuine connection.

Every question in Beside is designed to help you see into your child's heart. To understand their fears, their dreams, their sense of humor, and their growing identity. These aren't just conversation starters— they're bridges to deeper relationship.

"Be beside them. Not just physically present, but truly there—listening, understanding, and connecting."
